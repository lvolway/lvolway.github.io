Tasting Room

Our tasting room is open Wednesday - Monday from 9am - 5pm. Take a look at the wines available for tasting below.

Private Stock Wines

White Wines

Serve chilled with seafood and poultry.

  • Hillside White

    Rich, full bodied, and semi-dry. A blend of French Columbard, Muscat, and Chenin Blanc.

  • Chardonnay

    Rich fruit flavor with buttery, toasty oak aromas to produce a excellent balance. It remains the most popular white wine.

  • Champagne

    Extra Dry = Slightly Sweet. Excellent for that festive occasion.

Red Wines

Serve with pasta, meats or whatever.

  • Hillside Red

    Fine, dry, full-bodied red wine. A blend of wines pressed from red varietal grapes.

  • Cabernet Sauvignon

    The king of wines. Complex, full-bodied with rich oak overtones.

  • Zinfandel

    Rich fruit flavor, full of berry and peppery flavors along with oak barrel aging.

Reserve Selection Wines

Limited quantities of select vintages from unique locations of our vineyards which display exceptional quality and complexity. Wines included are:

  • Late Harvest Muscat

    Crystallized ginger, raw honey, and orange blossom in perfect balance. Enjoy after dinner as a dessert or paired with fruit desserts.

  • Late Harvest Zinfandel

    Bright and nimble on the palate. Sweet ripe plum, black cherry conserves and touches of hard spices.

  • Valdigue'

    Aromatics of strawberry and fresh herbs. Focused flavors of tangy cranberry and earth that can be accented by white pepper notes.

  • Petite Sirah

    Dry with flavors of black pepper, blackberries, blueberries, spice and licorice. Best paired with meats.

  • Sangiovese

    Savory wine that exhibits cherry flavors with more subtle notes of tomato. Includes earthy aromas and tea leaf notes

  • Sandstone Cabernet Sauvignon

    Dry wine that exhibits ripe fruit notes of blackberry, black currant, plum, and black cherry.

  • Sand Rock Hill Zinfandel

    The primary flavors are jam, blueberry, black pepper, cherry, plum, boysenberry, cranberry, and licorice.

  • Vintage Zinfandel Port

    Displays aromas of raisined berry fruit, sugared dates, chocolate, caramel and orange peel.
