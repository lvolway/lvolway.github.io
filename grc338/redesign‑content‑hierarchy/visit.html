Private Stock Wines

White Wines

Serve chilled with seafood and poultry

  • Hillside White
    Rich, full bodied, and semi-dry. A blend of French Columbard, Muscat, and Chenin Blanc.
  • Chardonnay
    Rich fruit flavor with buttery, toasty oak aromas to produce a excellent balance. It remains the most popular white wine.
  • Champagne
    Extra Dry = Slightly Sweet. Excellent for that festive occasion

Red Wines

Serve with pasta, meats or whatever

  • Hillside Red
    Fine, dry, full-bodied red wine. A blend of wines pressed from red varietal grapes.
  • Cabernet Sauvignon
    The king of wines. Complex, full-bodied with rich oak overtones.
  • Zinfandel
    Rich fruit flavor, full of berry and peppery flavors along with oak barrel aging.

Reserve Selection Wines

Limited quantities of select vintages from unique locations of our vineyards which display exceptional quality and complexity. Wines included are:

  • Late Harvest Muscat
  • Late Harvest Zinfandel
  • Valdigue'
  • Petite Sirah
  • Sangiovese (small clusters, Brunello Clone)
  • Sandstone Cabernet Sauvignon
  • Sand Rock Hill Zinfande
  • Vintage Zinfandel Port
