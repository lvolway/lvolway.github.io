Introduction

Wonderful Weddings

This site will serve as a portfolio and business site for the events company "Wonderful Weddings." There will be an about/landing page. There will also be a page with company employees bios and a services page that gives a brief overview of the services the company offers. Then there will be a gallery page with examples of previous events and finally, a contact page for inquiries.

Target Audience

The target audience for "Wonderful Weddings" is adults, mostly women, from ages of 20 to 40 who are looking to find a wedding planner for their big day. On this site, people will be able to meet the team, see examples of their work, and contact the company about future bookings.
